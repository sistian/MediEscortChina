Our Story

From Personal Experience to Professional Service

MediEscortChina was born from a simple observation: international patients were struggling to navigate China's excellent but complex healthcare system. Language barriers, unfamiliar procedures, and lack of local knowledge prevented many from accessing world-class treatment at a fraction of Western costs.

Founded in Wuhan — one of China's major medical research hubs — we set out to bridge this gap. Today, we connect patients from over 30 countries with China's top Grade-A Class-3 hospitals, providing end-to-end support from initial consultation to post-treatment recovery tourism.

Our team combines medical industry expertise with deep local knowledge, ensuring every patient receives personalized care, transparent pricing, and seamless logistics.
