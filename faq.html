Frequently Asked Questions About English Medical Escort in China

Everything you need to know about English hospital escort and medical interpreter services for foreign patients.

Getting Started

How do I start? What's the first step?

Simply contact us via WhatsApp (+86-155-2777-1775) or email. Share your medical needs and preferred dates. We'll provide a free consultation and recommend the best hospitals and treatment plans.

Cost

How much can I save compared to the US or UK?

On average, patients save 50-90% on medical costs. For example, a comprehensive health checkup costs ~$170 in China vs. $6,500 in the USA. Dental cleaning is ~$30 vs. $225. Exact savings depend on the treatment.

Safety

Are Chinese hospitals safe and reliable?

Absolutely. We work exclusively with Grade-A Class-3 hospitals (China's highest tier). These hospitals meet international standards, use state-of-the-art equipment, and many are JCI-accredited. Chinese doctors are highly trained and perform millions of procedures annually.

Language

Will language be a barrier? Do doctors speak English?

Most doctors in Tier-1 cities speak basic English, but not all are fluent. That's why we provide professional medical escorts who translate in real-time during consultations, help with hospital registration, and explain medications. You won't face language barriers.

Visa

What visa do I need for medical tourism in China?

Most patients apply for a standard tourist visa (L visa) or business visa (M visa). For extended stays, some hospitals can provide an invitation letter for a medical visa. We guide you through the entire visa application process.

Payment

How do I pay? What payment methods are accepted?

We accept PayPal, credit cards, Alipay, and WeChat Pay. We also support bank transfers. You pay directly to the hospital for medical services, and pay us separately for escort/translation fees. No hidden charges.

TCM

Can I combine modern medicine with Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM)?

Yes! Many patients choose to combine Western treatment with TCM for holistic recovery. We can arrange acupuncture, herbal medicine, and cupping sessions as part of your wellness package. This is a unique advantage of medical tourism in China.

Travel

Can I travel and sightsee during my medical trip?

Absolutely. Many procedures (checkups, dental, cosmetic) have short recovery times, leaving plenty of time for sightseeing. We can arrange guided tours to the Great Wall, West Lake, Panda Base, and more. We plan your itinerary around your medical appointments.

Insurance

Does my travel insurance cover medical treatment in China?

Most standard travel insurance covers emergency medical treatment but not elective procedures. Check with your insurer. We recommend purchasing comprehensive medical travel insurance that covers your specific treatment. We can recommend trusted providers.

Aftercare

What happens after I return home? Is follow-up included?

We provide detailed medical reports in English, translated prescriptions, and a follow-up plan. We can also coordinate with your home doctor via telemedicine. For some procedures, we recommend a brief follow-up visit before you fly home.

Still have questions?

Contact us directly. We reply within 24 hours.
