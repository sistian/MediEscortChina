FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to speak Chinese?+

No. We offer three tiers of English-speaking medical escorts: Basic for routine visits, Premium for complex procedures, and Expert for critical treatments. All provide real-time translation throughout your journey — from hospital registration to doctor consultations, tests, and treatment. All medical reports are available in English.

How do I pay?+

We accept PayPal (sistian@qq.com), Alipay (sisi@zhiyooo.com), WeChat Pay, and international credit cards. You can pay in your home currency — we handle the conversion.

Do I need a visa?+

Most countries qualify for China's 144-hour visa-free transit or 15-30 day visa-free entry. For longer stays, we provide visa invitation letters. We guide you through the entire process.

Are the hospitals really good?+

Yes. We partner only with Grade-A Class-3 hospitals (China's highest tier). These are top-tier institutions with JCI accreditation, international-trained doctors, and the latest equipment. Many rank in the global top 100.

What if I only need appointment booking, not escort?+

Our Online Appointment Booking Service ($15) is perfect for that. You share your medical condition, we book the appointment with a Chinese-speaking doctor, and you visit the hospital on your own using translation apps. If you later decide you need an escort, you can upgrade anytime.

Are the prices final?+

All prices shown on our website are approximate estimates. Final costs depend on your specific condition, the hospital chosen, the city tier, and any additional services required. We provide a detailed, personalized quote after understanding your needs. No hidden fees — full transparency.
