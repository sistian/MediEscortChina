🩺 Am I Eligible for CAR-T Therapy?

CAR-T therapy is typically indicated for patients who have:

  • Relapsed or refractory large B-cell lymphoma after at least 2 lines of systemic therapy
  • Relapsed or refractory multiple myeloma after 4+ prior lines of therapy
  • Failed or relapsed after stem cell transplantation
  • Adequate organ function (heart, liver, kidney, lung)
  • No active infections or severe comorbidities

⚠️ Important: Eligibility must be confirmed by a hematologist/oncologist after reviewing your complete medical history, imaging, and pathology reports. Request a free eligibility assessment →
