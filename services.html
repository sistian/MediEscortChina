FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to speak Chinese to use your services? +

No. Our escorts are fluent in English and will handle all communication with hospital staff, doctors, and nurses on your behalf. You can focus on your health while we handle the language barrier.

Which hospitals do you work with? +

We partner with 32+ Grade-A tertiary hospitals across 15+ cities in China, including Peking Union, Shanghai Renji, West China Hospital, and many more. We match you with the best hospital for your specific condition.

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for standard appointments and 2-4 weeks for specialist or surgery consultations. For urgent cases, we can sometimes arrange same-day or next-day service.

What is included in the escort service? +

All escort tiers include: hospital navigation and registration, real-time translation during doctor consultations, prescription assistance, payment guidance, and WhatsApp support throughout your visit. Premium and Expert tiers add medical terminology expertise, report translation, and follow-up coordination.

Are there any hidden fees? +

No. We are completely transparent about our pricing. All medical costs are official hospital rates — we do not add any markup. You only pay for our escort/translation service and any optional add-ons you choose.
